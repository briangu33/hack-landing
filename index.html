About

hack lodge is an immersive and intimate week-long software development program for undergraduate students interested in programming. participants spend 9 days working intensely in teams on software projects of their choosing, with the ultimate goal of shipping a polished product by the end of the week. teams are free to continue work on their projects after the program.

participants come from a variety of technical backgrounds - from students with no formal experience to experienced developers. mentorship is provided.

the winter 2018 iteration of hack lodge was held in Boston, MA. check back for updates on future dates.
